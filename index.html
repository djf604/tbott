Mobile Grooming

Let us come to you!

The Van

A state of the art conversion with all the tools needed to make your pet look its very best. The Bark of the Town Mobile Pet Groomery has its own water, electricity, air conditioning, heat, grooming table, and dryer.

The Process

We will arrive at your home or place of business and groom your pet. The grooming appointment will last 60-90 minutes.

The Advantage

Mobile grooming will allow you and your pet the luxury of not driving to a grooming salon. Not waiting hours for the pet to be groomed. Excellent for older pets, pets with car sickness or pets with separation anxiety. Your pet gets 1 on 1 attention.
